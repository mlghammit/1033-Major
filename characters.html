Meet the Characters of "La La Land"

Get to know the souls who bring the heart of 'La La Land' to life.

Mia Dolan

Mia Dolan, portrayed by Emma Stone, is a hopeful actress caught between the disheartening realities of her dreams and the magic of unexpected opportunities. Her journey of self-discovery and love intertwines with Sebastian's, creating a mesmerizing dance of fate.

Sebastian Wilder

Ryan Gosling's Sebastian Wilder is a passionate jazz pianist who dreams of opening his own club to preserve the dying art of traditional jazz. His love for music and Mia becomes the melody and harmony of his life, driving his ambitions and shaping his path.
